About

GoodWeed is an online marketplace for Cannabis and CRM for Cannabis Sales. We supply our sellers a rich suite of tools to manage their cannabis sales, as well as listing their products on our marketplace, where buyers can purchase them directly.

So GoodWeed is an integrated CRM and marketplace for selling cannabis. If you sell cannabis, you might find using our system increases your sales.

Using GoodWeed, you can list your products, sell your products, buy products from others, manage your customers, locations, products, purchases, and orders. It is your all-in-one tool for managing your cannabis sales.
